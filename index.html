ABOUT US

Abhayada is a great platform for women to stand up for themselves and encourage others for the same. The complaint system is quite easy and exclusive so don't think twice before registering the complaint. It is the best platform to stay anonymous and get the work done. We act as an unbiased cover between complainee and workplaces. You can share your feelings on the blog page as it is pshycologocially proven that penning down a mishap is way more self-enhancing and provides deep contentment. YOU ARE NOT ALONE IN THIS! This platform also provides an "Institute Sign-up" option for all the institutions to collaborate with us. It will allow us to work with the Workplace Harassment complaints effectively.




OUR MISSION

To strive towards enabling women to achieve equality and equal participation in the workplace and outside. And thus providing them a convenient way of tackling harassment

OUR VISION

To withhold women's dignity and self-respect irrespective of their social status for a secure and relatively much more ethical workplace.
